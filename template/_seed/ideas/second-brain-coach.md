# Second-brain as a management coach

## The idea

Once the vault has enough 1-1 and career history, a skill could act as a coaching
layer — spotting patterns across [[jane-doe]] and other reports (recurring
blockers, stalled growth, overdue recognition) and proposing 1-1 talking points.

## Why it matters

Turns passive note storage into proactive management leverage.

## Open questions
- How much history before patterns are trustworthy?
- Keep it suggestion-only to avoid over-automating judgment?
